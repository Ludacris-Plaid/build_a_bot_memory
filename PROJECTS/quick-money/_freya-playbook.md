# The Freya Playbook — Income Replacement via Social Media

**Core idea:** If you lost your job tomorrow, start a fresh Instagram account (spare email, nobody knows) and begin immediately.

This is the exact same thesis as Indications Media's quick-money strategy — low startup, fast execution, use existing platforms.

## Our Version

| Their Approach | Our Twist |
|---------------|-----------|
| Instagram fresh account | **Automated scraper + n8n pipeline** |
| Manual content creation | **AI-generated content + autopilot** |
| Time-consuming outreach | **SendGrid + Telegram bot delivery** |
| Build audience slowly | **Skip audience — sell data/results directly** |

## The Key Insight

Don't build audience. **Build pipeline.** Freya's advice works for individuals. For Indications Media, we scale it: one automation serves 100 clients instead of building 100 accounts.
