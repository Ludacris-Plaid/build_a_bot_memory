# Lead Generation & Advertising — Free / Cheap Methods

Brand everything under **Indications Media**. Every post, every comment, every listing.

---

## 1. Classifieds (Free)

| Platform | Category to Post In | Frequency | Limits |
|----------|-------------------|-----------|--------|
| **Kijiji** | Business Services, Computer Services, Real Estate Services | Daily | 1-2/day per IP |
| **Craigslist** | Services → Computer / Data / Web | Every 48h | Need flag management |
| **Facebook Marketplace** | Services → Business / Tech | Daily | Linked to FB account |
| **Facebook Groups** | RE Investor groups, Small Biz groups, Entrepreneur groups | 3-5/day | Group-dependent |

**Strategy:** Post don't sell — *educate.* "I scrape Zillow daily and send agents fresh leads for $47/mo here's how it works" performs way better than "Web scraping services $97."

**Automation opportunity:** n8n + browser auto-poster → schedule daily posts across platforms.
