## 🛡️ Protecting Against "Power Users" (Risk Management)

If one customer goes crazy and uses 100M tokens/month, your cost jumps to ~$12.60/month. Still profitable at $49, but less so. Here's how to handle it:

### Option 1: Soft Limits with Alerts
- Include a generous usage cap (e.g., 20M tokens/month)
- Send an alert to both you and the customer when they hit 80%
- Offer to upgrade them to a higher tier

### Option 2: Unlimited with Fair Use Policy
- "Unlimited" for normal business use
- Define "normal" as < 30M tokens/month
- If they exceed it, have a conversation about custom pricing

### Option 3: Pre‑Paid Token Packs (Add‑On)
- Offer extra tokens at $10/10M tokens
- This keeps power users profitable and gives you additional revenue
